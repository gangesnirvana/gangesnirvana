About Ganges Nivarna Community in Varanasi

Who are we?
A home in Varanasi
Ganges Nirvana is the place in Varanasi for artists and travelers to develop their creativity while living in community.

THE SPACE
A continious transformation
Shashwat decided to transform his house into an open space to welcome travallers willing to create a magic corner in Varanasi, even in the world.
Its athmosphere and walls are in constant transformation as their inhabitants are.
Its occupants mould its shape as the place also make every individual to evolve.
It is an endless metamorphosis!

get inspired by our members- awesome stories

BE OUR GUEST
And part of the family
Live in a community can be an incredible experience to open your mind and understand a different way of living. But sometimes some visitors don't have the time or just don't feel like gettig fully involved with the daily tasks.
Don't worry, you are also welcomed!
Ganges Nirvana reserves two bedrooms for having you as a guest. Visit Varanasi during the day and relax at home during the evening or join the dancing, massaging or whatever is going on!

Imagine exploring heart of the country by living in the core of its capital and that too in a dignified atmosphere, friendly service and homely comfort, all decorated by the amazing graffiti work around the walls by the travellers from around the globe. A home away from home - Ganges Nirvana, is the place for gypsy souls to enjoy and experience the cultural heritage of Varanasi. It is within walking distance of 5 minutes from Manduadih railway station. It is just 15 minutes away from Dashasumedh Ghat (main ghat, old city center) and is reachable with a shared tuk-tuk for just 20 rupees. Peaceful neighbourhood away from craziness and noise of the city (specially away from Honking) Cozy mattress, basic shared room matress in the community or private rooms in the home stay, basic kitchenette, reliable Wi-Fi, friendly and immensely helpful Host, clean and hygienic washrooms, safe arrangements and pure drinking water- all these make it a perfect spot for relaxation & good sleep after exploring heart and soul of the Holy & ancient city.

Namastè everyone, i'm Shashwat. I have been working in a 5 star hotel for several years, but after travelling to places like Auroville and exploring my own and neighbour country, I've experienced a great transformation and now I'm working on opening a Community International Youth Hostel. I like to say that I'm a gypsy traveller. In Feb 2016 I quit my Hotel job to chase my dream of exploration. Thanks to my itchy feet and to the abundance that is delivered when we drop preconceptions I had a lot of incredible adventures around india. I love nature, sustainability, outdoor and hiking, and I never stop dreaming. We are creating a new community hostel located in Varanasi very near to Manduadih Railway Station where all the train from New Delhi reaches. A couple of years back we found this beautiful old traditional Indian house with its lovely small garden in front. Since then we are putting a lot of efforts to turn this place into a lovely home for travellers and ourselves. The idea is not just about providing a place to stay for travellers, its more about creating a home for travellers made by travellers and artists livings space, where everybody can do and learn some form of Indian art and music make themselves feel comfortable, share their spirit, peace and love with others and and and..
